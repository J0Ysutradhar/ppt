# Glossary / Terminology

## Frequency Separation
Advanced retouching technique separating texture and color layers for natural skin editing.

## Dodge & Burn
Technique used to sculpt and enhance depth by selectively brightening and darkening areas.

## Color Grading
Adjusting tones and colors to create cinematic or artistic looks.

## Photo Compositing
Combining multiple images into one final image.

## White-Label Editing
Editing work delivered under the client agency’s branding.

## VFX
Visual effects such as smoke, fog, particles, flares, and overlays.

## Turnaround Time
The delivery time between receiving files and delivering edited photos.
